<div align = "center">

<h3>The Fibonacci sequence goes as follows:</h3>
<p><em>0, &nbsp;1,&nbsp;1,&nbsp;2,&nbsp;3,&nbsp;5,&nbsp;8,&nbsp;13,&nbsp;21,...</em>and so on</p>
